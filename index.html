About

I'm a product leader focused on turning AI/ML research into products that enterprises actually adopt. My work lives at the intersection of technical depth and strategic clarity: defining what to build, why it matters, and how it reaches customers.

"Good AI products don't just produce outcomes , they earn trust, reduce friction integrating into existing workflows, and get measurably better with every interaction."

Currently working on a domain-specific Generative AI platform delivering hyper-personalized outcomes for enterprises. I care deeply about product rigour: clear success metrics, well-defined evaluation and quality assurance processes, and systems that are explainable by design.
